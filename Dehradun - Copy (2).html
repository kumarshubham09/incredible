DEHRADUN

Dehradun- Treat for nature lovers

Dehradun is among the famous holiday destinations in north India for families as well as couples. The popular hill retreat is known for its awesome surrounding beauty, Doon School, Forest Research Institute, and Indian Military Academy.

Key attractions: Shikhar Fall, Guchhupani, Khalanga War Memorial, and Sahastradhara

How to reach:Dehradun has its own railway station and an airport that goes by the name of Jolly Grant.
